I. Purpose

Customer and Terminus share an interest in supporting onshore, defense-grade manufacturing of critical grid components and recognize the importance of strengthening U.S. transformer supply resilience. This NBPO expresses Customer's intent to reserve production capacity for future procurement once Terminus' facility becomes operational.

III. Commercial Understanding

This NBPO is non-binding and does not constitute a purchase order or contract. Terminus agrees to notify Customer upon commissioning of its Texas manufacturing facility and provide firm technical and commercial quotations. Customer intends, in good faith, to evaluate Terminus' transformers for inclusion in upcoming projects within the stated timeframe.

IV. Cooperation & Next Steps

Customer and Terminus will cooperate to refine technical requirements and commercial terms. Terminus will provide design datasheets, manufacturing updates, and factory-readiness milestones. Both parties agree to explore potential state or Federal incentives related to domestic sourcing.

V. Term

This NBPO remains effective for 36 months from the date of submission, renewable by mutual agreement.
